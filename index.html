How this estimate works

The probability is a heuristic combining: (1) a base risk floor, (2) time-of-day progression so tension builds through the day non-linearly, (3) recency of the last siren with exponential decay, (4) distance from the last siren to your location (closer = stronger effect), and (5) a clustering bonus when multiple alerts occurred recently. The result is smoothed and clamped between 0.5% and 99.4%.

Data from Pikud HaOref, updated by GitHub Actions every 5 minutes. When you see "Demo data", the workflow may not have run yet or data is empty. Not an official alert system.
